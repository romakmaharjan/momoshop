Momo Menu

1

Jhol momomomo with soup

Jhol basically means liquid and Jhol momos are the momos that are drowned in a bowl full of sauce called jhol achhar.

2

Kothey Momo

Kothey momo are half fried and half steamed momo which are made in an elongated shape.

3

Chilly MomoC Momo

It is a regular momo dipped in a hot and spicy sauce.

4

Paneer momo

This type of momo is filled with either Paneer, Khuwa, or Cheese and is served with achar. It is a very tasty momo.

5

Tandoori momo

Tandoori momo is a lip-smacking combo of momo and tandoori. It has a rich texture, and your taste bud will definitely drool over it.

6

Open Momo

The momos are like open flowers and you can pick and choose the sauces to fill them with. Usually, there will be around four sauces accompanying the open momos.

7

Green Momo

The cabbage for this spinach or green momos is made of spinach blended with flour and rub with the water from the spinach.
